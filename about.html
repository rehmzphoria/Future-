About Us


Future Plc is is a Public Limited Company aimed at giving the nation financial safety. We work in partnership with the Indonesian Bureau of Investigation to combat money laundering, tax evasion, fraud and other financial crimes. We also provide loans and incentives for the people. We offer courses on financial literacy for free and we have different articles on the financial attack on the nation dating back to 1955


Our motto is uplifting the world from one nation to another. We are ranked among the top 2 best institutions in Indonesia and the top 100 best financial institutions in Asia. We are also on partnership with Hyundai Motors and Samsung Finance
